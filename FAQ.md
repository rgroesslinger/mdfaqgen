## What are the primary colors in the RGB color model?

The primary colors in the RGB (Red, Green, Blue) color model are:
- **Red** (R)
- **Green** (G)
- **Blue** (B)

These colors can be combined to create a wide range of colors in digital displays.

## What is the chemical symbol for gold?

The chemical symbol for gold is **Au** (from the Latin 'aurum').

<a name="who-painted-the-mona-lisa"></a>
## Who painted the Mona Lisa?

The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo da Vinci between 1503 and 1519. It is currently housed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.
The painting is famous for its enigmatic smile and innovative technique. It is also one of the most visited and valuable artworks in the world.

## How many bones are in the adult human body?

The adult human body has approximately **206 bones**.
This number can vary slightly between individuals.

## What is the tallest mammal?

The tallest mammal is the **giraffe**, which can reach heights of up to 18 feet (5.5 meters).
